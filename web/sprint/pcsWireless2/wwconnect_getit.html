Getting the Wireless Web Connection Kit
To use Sprint PCS Wireless Web Connection, you'll need a wireless Internet ready Sprint PCS Phone and its corresponding Sprint PCS Wireless Web Connection Kit.
The Wireless Web Connection Kit available for $99.99 includes:
  • a data cable for connecting your Sprint PCS Phone to your computer,
  • a CD-ROM with installation, connection, maintenance, and troubleshooting software,
  • a user guide and
  • a cable adapter for connecting handheld and palm-sized computers.
You can purchase a Wireless Web Connection Kit by visiting our online store, visiting your nearest Sprint PCS Center, or calling 1-888-253-1315. No sign up is required to use Wireless Web Connection. However, the Sprint PCS Wireless Web Option offers a simple package that is more economical than the default per minute rate.
