About Sprint PCS Business ConnectionSM

With Sprint PCS Business Connection, you have the ability to access important corporate information such as email, calendar, contacts and documents -- all from your mobile device! Whether you're using a Sprint PCS Phone, Personal Digital Assistant (PDA) or any computer with a standard Internet browser, you can immediately connect to your corporate network, securely and in real-time, no matter where you are on the Sprint PCS Nationwide Network.

Sprint PCS Business Connection is comprised of two solutions - personal and enterprise. Depending on your individual or corporate needs, select the one that's right for you.
