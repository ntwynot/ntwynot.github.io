Sprint PCS Wireless Web Sites - Sports
Book4golf.com A national golf course information database and directory, with real-time tee-time reservations at participating golf courses.
CBS.Sportsline.com With CBS.SportsLine.com you'll get instant access to the most up-to-date sports coverage. Users will be able to follow their favorite sports on their Sprint PCS phones with the latest scores, news, standings, schedules, transactions and injury reports, as well as fantasy information, including live scoring, stats, standings and schedules. CBS.SportsLine.com is published by SportsLine.com, Inc. (NASDAQ: SPLN), the leading global Internet sports media company.
eGolfScore Wireless Scoring - Score & track your golf game! Analyze stats! Access scoring history! Broadcast your game in real-time via the web!
Go2online.com Go2™ provides the most comprehensive location-based directory and locator system designed for Internet and portable electronic devices, especially Web-enabled mobile phones, available to online and wireless users.
PGA.com This new service from PGA.com lets you view all your favorite PGA golf news and scores from across the country now on the Sprint PCS Network. PGA.com connects golfers from around the world to the more than 25,000 PGA Professionals for the PGA Experience.
VegasInsider.com With VegasInsider.com you'll have the latest odds, scores, handicapping analysis, and much more at your fingertips.
Yahoo! Use Yahoo! on your Sprint PCS Phone to get:
  • Access to your own MyYahoo! e-mail, calendar, and address book,
  • Stock price updates and financial market information,
  • Scores and news for your favorite sports teams,
  • Weather for many locations, domestic and international, and
  • The latest headlines and news.
