  1. The computer does not recognize your Sprint PCS Phone

    2. possible diagnoses   possible solutions
    Your Sprint PCS Phone is not communicating properly with your computer.   Without disconnecting the data cable, turn your Sprint PCS Phone off, then back on again.
    Your Sprint PCS Phone is not turned on.   Turn on your Sprint PCS Phone.
    The cable from the Sprint PCS Phone to the computer is not correctly connected.   Check to make sure you are using the correct data cable and check both ends of the cable for proper connections.
    You have two or more modems set up on your computer and your software is set up to use a modem other than the Sprint PCS Phone.   Configure your communications software so that it addresses the Sprint PCS Phone, rather than a different modem.
    Your data cable is damaged.
    OR
    Your data cable adapter is damaged.     		  Check the pins (metal contacts) on either end of the cable and/or the adapter. If any pins appear bent or out of line, you may require a new cable and/or adapter. Contact Sprint PCS for information on acquiring a replacement cable and/or adapter.
    Your Sprint PCS Phone has not been upgraded to be wireless Internet ready.   Contact Sprint PCS for instructions on upgrading your phone.
    Your Sprint PCS Phone is not wireless Internet ready.   Look for your phone on our wireless Internet ready Sprint PCS Phone list. If your phone is not wireless Internet ready, contact Sprint PCS for instructions on acquiring a wireless Internet ready phone.
    Other software is using the COM port.   Identify which software is using the COM port, then deactivate it.

    Return to Top

  8. You cannot establish a connection with the number you are dialing

    9. possible diagnoses   possible solutions
    You have two or more modems set up on your computer and the software is using a modem other than the Sprint PCS Phone.   Configure your communications software so that it addresses the PCS phone, rather than a different modem.
    You are dialing the wrong number.   Check the number, including the area code. Contact your service provider to verify the number.
    You are using a dialing prefix, such as a 9, before the phone number. Remove any dialing prefixes from the phone number.   The modem you are calling is out of service. Contact your service provider to verify the status of their server and modems.
    You are outside the service areas for your phone.   Raise the antenna on your Sprint PCS Phone. If possible, move to a location within a Sprint PCS Service Area. If you can’t place a voice call from your current location, then you will be unable to place a data call.
    Your user name is incorrect.   Check that you have entered the proper user name for your service provider.
    Your password is incorrect.   Check that you have entered the proper password for your service provider.

    14. possible diagnoses   possible solutions

    Return to Top

  14. Faxes are incomplete

    15. possible diagnoses   possible solutions
    Your fax application is not set to use Hardware Flow Control.   Within your fax application, check that Hardware Flow Control is turned "On." Consult the user’s manual for your fax software for more specific instructions.
    The modem is not set to use Hardware Flow Control.   Turn on Hardware Flow Control for the wireless modem. To do this:
    From the Start menu select "Setting", then "Control Panel".
    Double-click "modems" to open the Modem Properties window.
    Select "Qualcomm CDMA Wireless Modem" from the list, then click "Properties".
    Click the "Connection" tab.Click "Advanced..."
    Place a check mark in the box next to "Use flow control".
    Select "Hardware (RTS/CTS)".
    Click "OK".

    Return to Top

  26. The installer is searching for my phone, but it gets stuck while searching on a different COM port.

    27. possible diagnoses   possible solutions
    One of your COM ports is not responding to the installer.   Use the Windows Device Manager to verify that all COM ports are working properly. You may have to disable a COM port to proceed. Alternatively, if you know which COM port you are using for your Sprint PCS Phone, you can click the "Advanced" button to skip the COM port search.

  27. The software won’t make copies of my Dial-Up Networking Connections

    28. possible diagnoses   possible solutions

  28. Two area codes appear in the phone number when I try to connect.

    29. possible diagnoses   possible solutions
    The area code was improperly entered into your Dial-Up Networking connection.   Open your Dial-Up Networking window.
    Right-click on the connection that has the problem, then select "Properties."

    Edit the phone number so that the area code and telephone number appear in their respective boxes instead of in the same box.

    Return to Top

  29. I can’t connect to America Online using the Sprint PCS Dialer

    30. possible diagnoses   possible solutions
    America Online does not use Dial-Up Networking.   You must connect using your America Online software, not the Sprint PCS Dialer. Consult your User Guide to learn how to set up and use America Online with Sprint PCS Data.

    Return to Top

  30. America Online tells me that it can’t find my Sprint PCS Phone.

    31. possible diagnoses   possible solutions
    America Online cannot automatically detect your Sprint PCS Phone.   You must set up your America Online software to work with your Sprint PCS Phone. Consult your User Guide to learn how to set up and use America Online with Sprint PCS Data.

    Return to Top

  31. The Sprint PCS Dialer won’t save my user name.

    32. possible diagnoses   possible solutions
    You haven’t yet successfully connected.   Once you have successfully connected, then your user name will be saved and will appear automatically the next time you use that connection.

    Return to Top

  32. The Sprint PCS Dialer doesn’t save my password.

    33. possible diagnoses   possible solutions
    You chose to not save your password for this particular Dial-Up Networking connection.   Open the Dial-Up Networking window.
    Double-click the connection that’s not saving the password.
    Check the box marked "Save password", then click "Connect."
    Note that the "Save password" setting will be saved only once you have made a successful connection.

    Return to Top

  33. The Pre-call check tells me that the attached device may not be Sprint PCS-compatible.

    34. possible diagnoses   possible solutions
    Your Sprint PCS Phone is not properly responding to your computer.   Turn your Sprint PCS Phone off, then turn it back on again. Click the phone button in the Connect window to re-run the Pre-call check.

    Return to Top

  34. Parts of the Sprint PCS Dialer window are missing or invisible.

    35. possible diagnoses   possible solutions
    Your custom Windows settings for fonts sizes, menu sizes, et cetera are preventing the Sprint PCS Dialer from displaying all its contents in its small window.   Open your Control Panel (under the Start Menu, choose Settings, then Control Panel).
    Double-click Display, then select the "Appearance" tab.
    Choose "Windows Standard" from the "Scheme" pop-up menu to reset all windows to their default appearance settings. You must exit and then restart the Sprint PCS Dialer for the necessary changes to take effect.

    Return to Top

  35. Every call attempt fails and my Sprint PCS Phone says "DISCONNECTED - SYSTEM ABORT."

    36. possible diagnoses   possible solutions
    You are not authorized to place wireless web calls on the Sprint PCS Nationwide Network.   Please call Sprint PCS Customer Care at 1-888-298-0756.
